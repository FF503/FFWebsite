    We Are 503!

    We are FIRST Robotics Team 503 from Novi, Michigan. Our family of frogs includes Jr.FLL, FLL, and FTC teams as well as our FRC "force" and extends out to our mentors and alumni. This lifecycle perpetuates the growth of the program wherever our frogs call home. We are identified by our work ethic, commitment to character and our "hoppy-go-lucky" attitude!

    Recent News

     

    Frog Force hosted its sixth annual NBot community information night on June 1st, 2017. The purpose of this town hall meeting is to educate the community on the FIRST in Michigan Progression of Programs and how they can participate. Over 200 Novi students and parents attended. Frog Force President Shivani Bongu gave a presentation about FIRST robotics with information about each level of the Progression of Programs. If you couldn't attend the meeting, HERE is a copy of the presentation shown. If you are interested in participating in a FIRST team, please complete the NBot Interest Form. For more information contact frogforce@frogforce503.org.
