Robots in the "D"!

On the evening of August 22, 2012, at the sold-out Detroit Tigers vs. Toronto Blue Jays game, Team 503 brought major league baseball and robotics together in an event we call FIRST Pitch. Over 40,000 fans, including over 1,000 members of Michigan FIRST teams enjoyed a night of baseball and robot demonstrations. The highlight of the evening was a ceremonial first pitch delivered by our student-built robot, Cyber Young, to Paws, the Detroit Tiger’s mascot.
